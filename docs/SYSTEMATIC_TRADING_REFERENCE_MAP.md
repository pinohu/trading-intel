# Systematic Trading Reference Map

The platform uses `wangzhe3224/awesome-systematic-trading` as a free public reference map, not as vendored source and not as a trading engine.

## What It Adds

- A native checklist for data, alpha strategy, trading analysis, backtesting, live trading, architecture, tools, research resources, and AI/LLM research lanes.
- Fusion Alpha pressure that rewards complete evidence and penalizes missing proof before a setup is treated as decision-ready.
- Trust Matrix language that keeps durable outcome proof tied to slippage, fees, market regimes, data freshness, and engine coverage.
- Dashboard visibility through Research Stack readiness, Engine Fusion Map, and the Repo Features section.

## Boundary

This integration does not create a worker URL, broker adapter, market-data feed, or autonomous execution path. It only maps missing proof and research coverage. Live or paper execution still requires the app's normal ticket, risk, acknowledgement, broker, audit, and live-agent gates.

## Decision Rule

A trade idea should not graduate from watch to proven unless the relevant systematic lanes are visible:

- Data source and freshness.
- Alpha thesis and factor evidence.
- Trading analysis and catalyst context.
- Cost-aware backtest and validation proof.
- Live-control, pre-trade, and audit gates.
- Architecture and operations readiness.
- Tooling/alert path state.
- AI/LLM challenge output when used.
